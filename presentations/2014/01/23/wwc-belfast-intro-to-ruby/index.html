WWC Belfast: Intro to Ruby

At the start of January I got the chance to present at the Belfast branch of Women Who Code.It was my first go at running a workshop style session where the audience had a go at coding and I did some live typing. Definitely harder to pull off than an all talking presentation but a great experience. There was a good turn out and it was great to meet other women working in firms around Belfast and studying IT at uni.

I’ve stuck the code up on github and the slides on slideshare so hopefully other people beyond the audience that night will find them useful.

BTW if your company needs a push to see the value of you presenting take a look at this brilliant post by Zach Holman
